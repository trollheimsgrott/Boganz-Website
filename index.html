ABOUT US

At BoganZ we aim to capture some of the original S.T.A.L.K.E.R vibes from the respective game series right into Sumrak's harsh Namalsk DayZ continent.

OUR HISTORY...THE BEGINNINGS...

Back in late 2020, Uncle Fester heard the old ARMA 2 map Namalsk was being ported to DayZ. Given his fondness for this terrain, he began developing backend scripts so he could launch a modded server on day 1 of the Namalsk terrain release. On December 4 2020, the legendary Aleutian archipelago terrain that is Namalsk was released, and that same day BoganZ (DayZ server) was officially launched accompanied by a discord server.

The server launched with minimal mods, which included Trader and Expansion. He felt expansion gave survivors access (at the time) to many innovative features such as helicopters and amphibious cars.

Within the Namalsk lore, there are many references to the legendary game series S.T.A.L.K.E.R. Moreover, Fester was impressed by one interesting DayZ project coming out of Russia - a group of devs bringing S.T.A.L.K.E.R content to the DayZ workshop. Once this was publicly released, and translation mods available, these mods were implemented into BoganZ.

After playing on the server for some months, Trollheimsgrott began collaborating in mid to late 2021, and work began building server content which would create an immense sense of dread using diverse S.T.A.L.K.E.R content and scripts. Uncle Fester and Trollheimsgrott then began on their journey to make life on the archipelago that much harder.. a direct reference to the harsh gameplay in S.T.A.L.K.E.R.

This gives players oftentimes a need to band together and help eachother out, which promoted a friendly server, but hostily is aplenty as well!

GENERAL RULES

  1. Warnings - in General circumstance, three warnings are to be issued in writing to the player either ingame, or on Discord. For exceptional circumstances, any number of warnings can be circumvented at the Admin Discretion. After 3 warnings, you will be banned
  2. No disrespecting other players or staff members in or out of the game.
  3. No Hacking/Exploiting/Glitching.
  4. Do not threaten to DDoS/Dox players or the server.
  5. No combat logging.(Logging out within 5 minutes of being in combat.)
  6. No griefing other players while playing on the server.
  7. Roleplay including Rape or Sexual Assault is not tolerated. Violent prisoner roleplay of any sort must be consensual.
  8. If you lose the key to your vehicle as a result of combat, know that your locked car is vulnerable to theft anytime.
  9. Do not leave your vehicle inside Safe Zones, and log off. It's not a car park, and may be deleted or stolen.
  10. No hate speech, racism, abusive or aggressive speech, verbal or written, at any time. THis will not be tolerated
  11. No killing or backstabbing other players while in discord calls/channels with said person(s).
  12. You are not to dismantle the walls or any building items of active bases just for the reason of stealing materials.
  13. The presence of an Admin anywhere is not a safety bubble, and does not necessarily guarantee that attacks by other players can't happen at the same time. Staff will however use their discretion and ask for hostilities to cease if necessary.
  14. Absolutely no Racism, Sexism will be tolerated, and will result in a ban.
  15. Players who choose to use offensive nicknames in game, will be given one warning, and one warning only. Inclusive but not limited to, hate speech, racism, sexually aggressive or sexist names.

TRADER / ITEM RULES

  1. If a loophole is found in the trader pricing it is to be brought to the attention of the Admin immediately, and not exploited. We can guarantee making it worth your while to report it, and action will be taken should you be found to be exploiting.
  2. Do not steal inside of traders
  3. Do not follow people out of traders to kill them
  4. Do not use Megaphones / hot mic in traders
  5. Camping Traders strictly prohibited - no exceptions - Admin to use discretion, providing video or image evidence is advised
  6. Vehicles left inside traders for extended periods of time (exceeding 24hr) will be relocated or destroyed
  7. Do not enter the safezone after 10 minutes of being in combat (with players)
  8. No stashes, safes or containers inside the safezone, these will be removed
  9. Item loss / replacement: It is the admin right to withhold any item replacement due to loss, lag, glitch, despawn, desync or other reasons. Video capture / steam screenshot is strongly advised to detail key information such as what happened, the nature of the incident and relevant information, specific persons / items and so on.

BASE BUILDING RULES

  1. 1 flag per group/solo, unless building inside one of the faction bases
  2. "No build" zones vary in size, but are usually around 250-500m from Traders, Military Zones, or other important Server Locations. See the ingame No Build Panel for information
  3. Do not build on roads
  4. Do not take over occupied bases. All occupied bases require atleast one standalone container with valuables inside first door. Raiding with this intent is prohibited.
  5. No inventory stacking (Deploying tents inside of tents)
  6. No unrealistic prop stacking to raid bases (This includes chopping down trees, and other forms of stacking)
  7. No blocking access to water pumps
  8. No blocking or restricting entrances to bases with objects, tents or barbed wire (there must be at least one entrance)
  9. No unrealistic building of bases, no floating platforms or floating ocean bases

DISCORD RULES

  1. No racist/violent hate speech allowed within the discord
  2. Keep discussions relevant and on topic for that channel
  3. No posting explicit pictures within the discord
  4. No joining voice chats and playing loud and obnoxious sounds/music/screaming while other players are present
  5. No advertising other discord / Dayz servers in discord without express permission from Administration
  6. There is to be no RP talk or discussion in the discord, it is this way to stop META gaming and to keep the discord somewhat cleaner, if you want to RP with someone, do it in game or in a private Discord Channel
  7. No linking to piracy or cheats

Another Update Coming, Wipe planned

Thursday, 15 September 2022

STALKERS: We are overdue for a change up, while work is underway to create (what is almost certainly) the most diverse, realistic and the most accurately balanced difficulty system across all zombies, on any server, we have geared up to switch up our main weapons pack. (It is time for a fresh change, and we believe for the better.) Having taken on board a suggestion from our buddy Jibblets, we will be putting to rest Teddy's Weapons Pack, and Advanced Weapon Scopes mods. We will be replacing them with Dead Air Weapons Pack which is actually created with S.T.A.L.K.E.R. enterprise in mind. The pack brings in a minimal approach when it comes to skins, Wooden and rustic, and has one of the most well balanced damage effects I have seen on a weapon pack. The difference between weapon types and callibres is uncanny. It is for this mod pack that we will need to perform a wipe to prevent item loss issues. I will be going out of town in the next day or so, and plan to switch over when im back in town. I fly back the 25th of September. This places a wipe likely sometime between 26th and 28th of September. Work will continue on the Zombie difficulty at this time also.
